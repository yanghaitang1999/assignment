The main ingredients of hot pot are boiled raw materials in the pot. According to the nature of the raw materials, it can be divided into seafood raw materials, river raw materials, poultry raw materials, livestock raw materials, vegetables and fruits raw materials, raw products and so on. Almost all the raw materials that can be used to make dishes can be used as the main ingredients of hot pot.
Hot pot soup is the bottom of the pot soup, the most commonly used is red soup, followed by white soup. Red soup is spicy soup, with thick soup and pepper, bean paste, fermented black beans, glutinous rice wine, rock sugar, refined salt, rice wine, a variety of spices and so on. White soup juice uses the soup juice that the elbow of bone of old hen, fat duck, pig, ham, pig lean meat, onion, ginger, wine waits for to boil, cooperate with red soup juice commonly use, rarely use alone, use also often to want to dip in some seasoning edible namely.
Dipping dish is an indispensable part of the dipping hot pot, the common dish has sesame oil flavor dish, garlic paste flavor dish, pepper oil flavor dish, red oil flavor dish, spicy sauce flavor dish, Sichuan Qi dipping dish, sauce flavor dish, chive flower flavor dish and so on. The bottom of the pot is heated to bring out all the characteristics of the seasoning. To adjust the taste of the bottom of the hot pot, we must make full use of the fat solubility and water solubility of condiments when heated, in order to achieve the "harmony of five flavors". This requires the operation of the correct control of the temperature, feeding sequence and boiling time, four or five kinds of flavor blend, the proportion is appropriate. Hot pot condiment is an organic combination of various condiments. There is a subtle relationship between the five flavors and the five flavors. Excessive amount of a certain condiment will cover up the flavor of other condiments, resulting in a "superfluous guest and dominant guest". Therefore, in the hot pot seasoning a variety of condiments to be appropriate than the column.
