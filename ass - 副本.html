Introduction

Hot pot, one of China's original delicacies, is a kind of food for all ages. Hot pot generally refers to the cooking method of cooking all kinds of food with the pot as the appliance, the heat source, the water or the soup to boil, and also refers to the pot and utensils used in this cooking method. Its characteristic is to cook while eating, or the pot itself has the effect of heat preservation, when eating the food is still steaming hot, soup and food together. Similar dishes are served all over the world, but are particularly popular in East Asia.
